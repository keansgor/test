About us

8Counts was set up in the year 2003 by Chartered Secretaries and affiliates that have been working with various corporate clients in Malaysia. It was initially established as a corporate secretarial provider and later ventured into licensing and other corporate compliances related services. Our efforts and contribution have earned us a reputation of being “efficient solution provider” among our clients.

We provide a full range of services to various type of companies; from company setup, corporate advisory, maintaining statutory records, outsourced accounting, audit, taxation, payroll and HR services, to strategic and transactional advice and regulatory support, we can provide you with the confidence and security that your business operations will have valuable independent scrutiny.
